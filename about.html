Brady Holdings is a privately held investment and holding office established to oversee the long-term interests of a family with roots in Ireland and ongoing ties to the United Kingdom.

The firm adopts a selective, long-term approach to capital stewardship, with an emphasis on preservation and continuity. Activity is limited and undertaken on an opportunistic basis, primarily through established relationships built over time.

Engagements are typically conducted passively and on a non-public basis.

The office is privately owned. Brady Holdings operates from the UK, with interests principally focused across the UK and Europe.

Brady Holdings is not authorised or regulated, does not provide investment advice, and does not accept or manage capital on behalf of third parties. The firm does not solicit external investment.
